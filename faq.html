What does Ologolo mean?

Like many other words, Ologolo means different things to different people.

According to Google translate, it means "sweeping" in Samoan.

Some may associate it with graphic design.

It might also mean that comming up with a sufficiently unique name on the internet is hard. Very, very hard.

Anyway... To us, Ologolo is home to a collection of open source software libraries written in Java.

How do I pronounce Ologolo?

We don't know. Some options are o-lo-go-lo, olo-gólo or o-lógo-ló. What do you think?
